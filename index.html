What's the deal?

This is the web page for Prince Protect, a game Dan wrote during the summer of 2012!

Okay, cool what's Prince Protect about?

Prince Protect is an arcade-style, boyfriend-defense, action game where players play as princesses, attempting to keep their harem of princes away from evil ninjas.

Can I play it with a friend?

Yes! It has two-player co-op with keyboard support. You might want to use Joy2Key to get gamepads going. That's more fun, in my opinion.
